Here's a stand-up comedy joke about cats:  

"Did you know cats are the only animals that will *intentionally* knock things off your desk, then sit on them like they’re trophies? I was trying to work on my taxes the other day, and my cat decided to do a *full-on conga line* across the keyboard. I looked up, and there he was—tail high, looking like he’d just won the Nobel Prize for *indifference*. I tried to yell, 'Get off my laptop!' but he just stared back with the eyes of a man who’s seen too much and knows the truth: you’re not his servant, you’re his *personal waiter, therapist, and janitor*. You’re not my friend, you’re my roommate, and I’m just here for the snacks and the lap space. That’s the deal."  

*(Pause for effect, then add with deadpan seriousness)*  
"Also, if you ever leave the room, they’ll *literally* meow at the door until you come back. It’s like they’re auditioning for a role in a horror movie titled *'The Human Scapegoat.'*"
