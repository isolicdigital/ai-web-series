Why did the cat bring a ladder to the party?  
Because it heard the guest of honor was a *mouse* and it wanted to *climb* the social ladder.  

But when the cat finally caught the mouse, it just sat there, licking its paws, and said, "You’re not the first, and you won’t be the last. I’ve got a *whole* list of people who need to be *dispatched*."  

And the worst part? The mouse didn’t even flinch. It just looked up and said, "Nice jacket."
