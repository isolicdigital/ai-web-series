**Setup:**  
"Why did the cat get kicked out of the house?"  

**Punchline:**  
"Because it was the only one who could knock over the vase without getting a reward."  

**Alternative version (more edgy):**  
"I adopted a cat, and now I'm a millionaire. The cat just sits there and stares at me, but I know it's plotting to take over the world. Because it's got a PhD in *'I'm not doing anything, but you're paying for it.'*"  

Either works—depends on how much you want to lean into the cat's perceived laziness.
