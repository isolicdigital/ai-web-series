Did you know cats are the only animals that will sit on your keyboard and then judge you for using it? Unlike dogs, who are happy to be your best friend, cats are like your ex who never sends a text but still expects you to check their Instagram. They’ll knock over your plants, steal your food, and then when you’re upset, they’ll rub against your legs like they’re trying to apologize with a paw print. So next time you’re sad, just remember: your cat’s love is conditional, but it’s always on your lap.
