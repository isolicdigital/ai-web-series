You know you're a cat person when you start to believe that your furniture is just a temporary base of operations for their global takeover. They’ve got their own version of the CIA, and their mission is to dismantle your carefully curated living space—starting with the vase you *vaguely* remember buying for its aesthetic value. By the time you realize your lamp is now a tomb for a dead mouse, you’re already halfway to accepting that the only way to survive is to join their cause. So when they knock over your prized collection of human-made objects, it’s not just a vase—it’s a declaration of war. And you’re the collateral damage.
