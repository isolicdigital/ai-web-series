Here's a stand-up comedy joke about cats:  

"_cats_ are like tiny, fluffy dictators. They walk into a room, sit on your keyboard, knock over your vase, and then stare at you like you're the problem. You try to clean up their chaos, but they’re already plotting their next move. And when you’re done, they’re like, 'Ah, you’ve done your duty. Now, let’s see… do you have a treat? Or are you just a human-shaped dustbin for my hairballs?'  

The best part? You’re not even allowed to call them ‘cute’ anymore. It’s *mandatory*. If you say ‘adorable,’ they’ll just flick their tail like, 'This is *so* overrated.' You’re stuck in a relationship where you’re the servant, the janitor, and the therapist—all while they nap in the sun, judging your life from a 30-foot high throne."  

*(Pauses, then deadpan)* "And if you ask them to stop scratching the couch, they’ll just look at you and say, 'You want me to stop *scratching*? What’s the alternative? You want me to *not* mark my territory? You’re asking for a rebellion.'"
