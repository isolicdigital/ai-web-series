Here's a stand-up comedy joke about instant noodle survival:  

"Instant noodles are the ultimate survival food. They’re like a bag of hope, a cup of water, and a desperate prayer to your taste buds. I once survived a week in a hotel room with nothing but a bag of instant noodles. The hotel provided the water, and I provided the courage. Turns out, the real survival is when you’re eating noodles and pretending you’re not starving. But here’s the kicker—when the apocalypse comes, and you’re scrambling to find food, you’ll be the one yelling, *'I’ve got the noodles! I’ve got the noodles!'* while the zombies gnaw on your soul. That’s the *true* survivalist meal: a single cup of broth and a lifetime of regret."  

*Punchline: "And if you’re lucky, the noodles will be *just* right. Or maybe they’ll be *completely* wrong. Either way, you’ll be alive. That’s the only thing that matters."*
